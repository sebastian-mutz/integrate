Credits¶

This is the credits section for the course material. We encourage people who continue development of this course to always include this section in derivatives and append a list of additional contributors in the acknowledgements table below. Note that derivatives have to be distributed under the same license. See the license section for more information.

Version History¶

Note that the exercises in the P1 and P2 series are based on the ClimStat exercise series developed as part of the University of Tübingen geoscience module “Climate Dynamics, Probability and Statistics”, which is still available here. It is therefore credited as version 0.9 in the INTEGRATE learning package.
